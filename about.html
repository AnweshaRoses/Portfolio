About Me

I am a passionate person who enjoys the creativity of working in tech, hence my love for making websites. I really enjoy working on different projects involving new technologies hoping that one day my work will have a direct impact on our lives. I also enjoy being surrounded by brilliant people that share the same vision and I am truly humbled by their support.

My Background

I was born and raised in India before moving to Singapore for my further highschool studies, after my graduation lockdown commenced due to covid-19, as a result I have been attending online classes ever since, that is when I discovered my passion for coding.

Intrigued by travelling, music, art and delicious food, seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for the things that are worth it and to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me, are the things I take forward in my career.

Current Education

I am currently completing my 3rd semester as a computer science undergrad at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology in Bhubaneswar, Odisha.
I am a part of two societies, namely 'Kamakshi he for she' which is a society focusing on bringing gender equality and 'KIIT- Animal and Environmental welfare society' as a writer and editor for their blog.
